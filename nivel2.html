Here you got some rules to level III

Comparatives of equality are used to express that two items have the same degree of a particular quality.

For countable items, "as many... as".

as many apples as → tan manzanas como

For non-countable items, "as much... as".

as much water as → tanta agua como

Example:

"I have as many friends as Sarah."
